Chulha Chauki ka Dhaba

Delicious Rajasthani Food At Rasovara, Lavelle Road Located right in the heart of Bangalore’s favourite spot UB City, Rasovara serves you delicious Rajasthani dishes oozing with authentic flavours. You can start your meal with the special Rajdhani Chai, then proceed to have the medium spicy, crunchy Bhindi Rajasthani, delicious Paneer Maharani and Mirch Makai Pulao. And when it comes to desserts they have plenty to offer like Apple Jelabi, Kesari Malpua and Sizzling Halwa. This is one of the best Rajasthani places in Bangalore that you must visit.
